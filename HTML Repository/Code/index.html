NBA Free Agency Signings

Buddy Hield goes to the Warriors!

Buddy Hield signs with the warriors in a block buster deal that includes Kyle Anderson coming to the bay and Klay Thompson going to the Mavericks. The deal was 2 years and 21 million dollars guaranteed, with him being allowed to stay for up to four yers before going back into free agency.

Lebron James will resign with the Lakers!

The Lakers will sign Lebron James for 2 years in a 100 million dollar deal. This will keep him to play with his son Bronny James and get a whiff at what might be his last year. Bronny James was the 55th pick in the NBA draft selected by The Lakers. Lebron will be entering his 22cd season.
